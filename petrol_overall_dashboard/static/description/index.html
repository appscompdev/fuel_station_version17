Fuel Station Management Dashboard Key Features

Dashboard Inventory Tracking for Fuel Categories: The dashboard of the fuel station management system allows for the maintenance and tracking of current stock levels for categories, enabling efficient inventory management.

Dashboard Tracking: Fuel Categories and Distilled Water Sales and Purchases: This dashboard enables tracking of sales and purchases for categories such as Petrol, Diesel, Oil, and Distilled Water, providing information for today, yesterday, and last week.

Comprehensive Dashboard: Shiftwise Income, Employee Tracking, and Sales History: The dashboard offers detailed insights into shiftwise income, employee tracking by department, and sales history, presented through graphical views. Users can also track purchase and stock details, with filter options available for easy analysis and navigation within the dashboard.

Product Page Details: Storable Type, Internal References, and Weight Calculation

The product master allows users to configure all necessary fuel category items, specifying their storable types for efficient inventory management.

Flexible Purchase Options: Configuring Staff Details in Employee Master with Specific Departments

The employee master facilitates the configuration of all staff details, including department assignments, ensuring efficient organization and management within the system.

Tracking Daily Fuel Nozzle Entries in Closing Entry for Comprehensive Reporting.

The closing entry captures day-to-day fuel nozzle entries, facilitating tracking in sales, purchase, inventory, expenses, and accounting modules. This data is also included in ledger reports for historical analysis and auditing purposes.

Activity-Based Dashboard: Fetching and Displaying Module Values with Filter Options

The dashboard dynamically fetches and displays values from various modules, presenting them in an attractive format with filter options for enhanced usability and analysis.
